# Preset Lifecycle

- Status: NOT_IMPLEMENTED

A preset progresses through concept, schema review, compatibility mapping, adapter validation, preview testing, isolated execution testing, postcondition validation, rollback testing, approval, and release. A stage may be denied or returned for correction.

Preset versions are pinned independently from modules, addons, protocols, and Realm Control. A preset cannot execute merely because its definition parses or carries a risk label.
