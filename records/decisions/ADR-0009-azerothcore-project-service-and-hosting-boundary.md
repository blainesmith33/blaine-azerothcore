# ADR-0009: AzerothCore Project Service and Hosting Boundary

- Status: Accepted at S0 — Proposed
- Date: 2026-07-14
- Scope: AzerothCore development project only

## Decision

This boundary applies to the AzerothCore project, not to blAIne as a whole. The project may produce and distribute original tooling, modules, addons, control-plane software, documentation, configuration, presets, automation, integration work, training, and support services.

The project does not provide hosted game realms. The project does not sell access to game realms. The project does not sell proprietary game content. Blizzard-owned client software, artwork, music, models, maps, data, and other proprietary assets are not project distribution artifacts.

Every commercial or public-release decision remains subject to component-level license and provenance review. Sanitization, attribution, upstream terms, and distribution rights must be evaluated before release.

## Boundary

This is an engineering and project-scope boundary, not legal advice. It does not redefine the broader blAIne umbrella identity or approve any commercial release.
